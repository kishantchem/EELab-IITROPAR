What we do

Our research group members are involved in both experiment and computational investigations.

Recovery of Valuable Metals

Recovering Lithium and Cobalt with high efficiency from spent LIBs.

Hydrogen Storage in MOFs

Designing MOFs to improve hydrogen storage capacity.

Gas Separation using Porous Materials

Using MOFs, zeolites, and porous carbons for CO₂, H₂, N₂, O₂, and CH₄ separation.

Gas Absorption using Solvents

Absorption using amines, ionic liquids, and DESs for CO₂/H₂S capture.
