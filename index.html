What we do

Our research group members are involved in both experimental and computational investigations.

Recovery of Valuable Metals

We develop methodologies to recover valuable metals like Lithium and Cobalt from spent LIBs with a leaching efficiency of ~99%.

Hydrogen Storage in MOFs

Designing advanced MOFs for high-capacity hydrogen storage for green fuel applications.

Gas Separation using Porous Materials

Gas separation using MOFs, zeolites, and porous carbons enables energy-efficient, high-purity gas production.

Gas Absorption using Solvents

We explore amine, ionic liquid (IL), and deep eutectic solvent (DES) systems for selective CO₂ and H₂S absorption.
